ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

Adaptive Path-finding Agent

Outcomes

  • Visualised reinforcement learning policies in a two-dimensional grid-world
  • Implemented teacher-student model
  • Analysed the impacts of variable availability and accuracy of the teacher advice on the student's rewards
PROJECT OVERVIEW
Understanding the key mechanisms of Reinforcement Learning
In the third term of 2024, I was dedicated to understanding the underlying concepts behind artificial intelligence. The two main topics of focus were artificial neural networks and reinforcement learning.
This project aimed to provide a deep understanding of the inner mechanisms facilitating a reinforcement learning agent and the policies guiding its actions. From this, we can take inspiration from real-world interactions in the form of a teacher-student model to train future agents. Analysis of this seeks to develop futher recognition of the impacts of balancing exploration and exploitation on an agent's success.
